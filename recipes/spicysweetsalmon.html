Spicy-Sweet Salmon

Description

Glazed salmon that is spicy and sweet!

  1. 1½ pounds fresh salmon fillet with skin removed
  2. ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  3. ¼ cup olive oil
  4. ¼ cup soy sauce
  5. ¼ cup water
  6. 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  7. ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  8. 1 teaspoon onion powder
  9. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  10. 2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
  11. ⅓ cup brown sugar, packed
  12. salt and ground black pepper to taste
